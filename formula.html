Inclination Angle

Accoding to the well known drilling data handbook (green book, page J4) calculation formula of an inclination angle depends on a ratio of a well horizontal displacment (D) and radius (R) of curvature of build-up section, but this ratio whether D > R or D < R is not really necessary to check.

Inclination angle (α ) can be obtained with a common solution formula for any displacment-radius ratio (see the derivation).
